What's it like Being an astronaut

Being an astronaut is intense and awe-inspiring. Training involves physical conditioning, simulations, and technical learning. Launching into space is thrilling, with powerful G-forces and weightlessness. Life on the spacecraft is structured, focused on experiments and maintenance. Seeing Earth from space is breathtaking, and spacewalks are exciting but risky.
