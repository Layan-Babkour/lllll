Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known place with life. It orbits the Sun every 365 days and is about 4.5 billion years old. Its atmosphere, mostly nitrogen and oxygen, protects life by regulating temperature and blocking harmful radiation.

Around 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water, mainly oceans, essential for life. The planet has diverse climates, from warm tropics to icy poles. While greenhouse gases keep Earth warm enough for life, human activity is causing climate change, affecting natural systems. Despite challenges, Earth remains unique in supporting life.
